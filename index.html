Game Mechanics

Action Points: Each character has a limited number of actions per turn. Movement and attacks consume action points.

Combat: Characters can attack enemies in adjacent tiles or use ranged attacks. Defense rolls can reduce incoming damage.

Vision: Enemies can detect characters through line of sight. Walls and doors block vision.

Group Management: You can have up to 3 characters in your group. Switch between them using Tab or the character panel.
